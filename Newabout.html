01. Our Founding Story Established in 2015, our journey began in the heart of Hebbal, Bengaluru, with a shared commitment to transform the workspace experience.

Fueled by a passion for innovation and a dedication to unparalleled service, our founders embarked on a mission to redefine the way individuals and teams connect with their ideal workspaces.

02.
The Early Days In the early days, our focus was clear: to create a vibrant community of like-minded individuals.

What started as a vision soon transformed into a reality, with each milestone marking a step toward realizing our commitment to excellence. As the first coworking space in North Bengaluru, we carved out a niche, growing steadily within the same premises.

03.
Evolving with Our Members As we grew, so did our understanding of the unique needs and aspirations of our members.

We evolved our services, adapting to industry trends while staying true to the core values that define us. From pioneering flexible and innovative workspaces to embracing sustainable practices, our journey has been one of continuous innovation and member-centric solutions. At our peak, we managed over 200,000 square feet of commercial real estate, our portfolio a testament to our relentless drive.

As we reflect on our founding history, we remain committed to the principles that have guided us from the beginning – integrity, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our story is one of evolution, adaptation, and an unwavering commitment to the future of work. Join us as we continue to shape the workspace of tomorrow.
