About Us

Welcome to Cospaze: Where Collaboration Meets Innovation

At Cospaze, we believe that the best work happens when we come together, share ideas, and support one another. That's why we've created a vibrant coworking community that brings together professionals from all industries and backgrounds.

Contact Us

Our Story: A Journey of Evolution and Adaptation

Our Mission: Empowering the Future of Work

We pioneered coworking in North Bengaluru in 2015, growing steadily before officially launching Cospaze in 2017. Since then, we've evolved and adapted, managing 200,000+ sqft of commercial real estate and navigating the 2020 lockdown. In 2022, we launched Desklinq, a platform connecting people with workspaces anywhere.
