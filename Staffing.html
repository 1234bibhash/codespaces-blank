The Most Common Questions

What is Cospaze?



Coworking is a shared office space where people from different companies or industries work together in a collaborative environment. Coworking spaces provide a professional workspace, amenities and a community of like-minded individuals.

What are the benefits of coworking?



Coworking provides many benefits such as cost savings, flexibility, networking opportunities, a creative and productive environment, access to resources and amenities, and a sense of community.

What amenities and services are included?



Coworking spaces typically provide amenities such as high-speed internet, conference rooms, private phone booths, printing and scanning facilities, kitchen and lounge areas, and access to events and workshops.

How do I become a member of a coworking space?



To become a member of a coworking space, you need to fill out an application form and select a membership level.
